The average cost of building a website in Pakistan can vary greatly depending on several factors, including:
  • Type of website: A simple brochure website will cost significantly less than a complex e-commerce platform.
  • Features and functionality: The more features and functionalities you need, the higher the cost will be.
  • Design complexity: A custom-designed website will be more expensive than one built using a template.
Here's a general range of what you can expect to pay for website development in Pakistan:
  • Basic website: Rs. 25,000 - Rs. 50,000 (e.g., a simple portfolio website with a few pages)
  • Business website: Rs. 50,000 - Rs. 150,000 (e.g., a website for a small business with more features and functionality)
  • E-commerce website: Rs. 150,000 - Rs. 300,000+ (e.g., an online store with product listings, shopping cart, and payment gateway)
    • Here are some additional factors to consider when budgeting for your website:
  • Domain name registration: This will typically cost around Rs. 1,000 - Rs. 5,000 per year.
  • Web hosting: This will depend on the size and traffic of your website, but expect to pay Rs. 5,000 - Rs. 20,000 per year.
  • Maintenance and updates: Once your website is launched, you'll need to budget for ongoing maintenance and updates. This can cost around Rs. 5,000 - Rs. 10,000 per year.
